Copen Connect:

Where we connect you to Copenhagen

Whether you're an expat, local, or visitor, our mission is to get you to the right quarters and among the right people to celebrate the vibrant Scandinavian spirit of Copenhagen like never before. Imagine enjoying a glass of white wine and smørrebrød on the streets of Nyhavn or marveling at architectural wonders like the Royal Danish Opera House. Or perhaps you'd prefer to cozy up in one of our member's flats for a true hygge experience.

Upcoming events

  • Copenhagen Stroll: Join us for a walk through Copenhagen’s historic quarters. Discover stories from the past and marvel at the architecture that shapes the city’s unique character, from the majestic Rosenborg Castle to the iconic Round Tower.
  • Seaside Soiree: Experience Copenhagen’s stunning waterfront with a special evening by the sea. Enjoy the fresh breeze, picturesque views of the Øresund Bridge, and conversation with fellow members as the sun sets over the harbor.
  • Nordic Nibbles Food Tour: Taste your way through Copenhagen’s culinary landscape with stops at some of the best eateries and food markets. Savor the local flavors and learn about the city’s gastronomic evolution, all while surrounded by the city’s chic and modern design.
  • Hygge Night at Freya’s: Embrace the Danish concept of hygge at our cozy night gatherings. Enjoy warm drinks, good company, and a relaxed atmosphere at our host Freya’s place, one of our founders, filled with the soft glow of candlelight and a lot of hygge.
